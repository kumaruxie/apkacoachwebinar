THE MAPPING TOOLKIT

The Four Relationship Personalities

Under stress, we revert to default conflict coping patterns. Understanding these patterns prevents minor disputes from escalating.

❤️

The Connector

Needs Reassurance
Core Emotional Need

Requires consistent reassurance, emotional validation, and constant check-ins to feel safe.

Stress Behavior Trigger

Triggered by emotional silence, sudden withdrawal, or a partner turning away to their screen.

Common Conflict Mistake

Pushes for immediate talks, demands answers, and accidentally increases pressure when their partner needs space.

Coaching Communication Tip

Allow them to express concern without defensiveness. Say: "I hear you, and I want to resolve this, but I need 10 minutes to process so we don't argue."
